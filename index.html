Privacy Policy

Last update: December 27, 2024

1. Introduction

FiziMove ("we", "us", "our", "app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the application processes your data when you use our mobile application.

2. Data Collection and Storage

The FiziMove application collects and processes data in the following way:

Data stored locally: All data entered by you in the application (discomfort/tension level, exercise history, favorite exercises, completed exercises, preferences) are stored exclusively locally on your device using local data storage (AsyncStorage) and are not synchronized with external servers.

Data collected by external services: The application uses the following external services that may collect data:

  • Google AdMob - advertising service that may collect device technical data, advertising identifiers (IDFA/AAID), data about ad interactions, and other data in accordance with Google AdMob privacy policy

3. Details of Collected Data

The application does not require login or account creation. We do not directly collect the following categories of data:

  • Account data - the application does not require login or account creation
  • Identity data - we do not directly collect email address, name, surname, or other personal data
  • Location data - the application does not have access to location

Note: External service (Google AdMob) may collect device technical data and advertising identifiers in accordance with Google's privacy policy.

4. Local Data Storage

All data entered by you in the application is stored exclusively on your device in local data storage. This includes:

  • Discomfort/tension level recorded by you
  • History of completed exercises
  • List of favorite exercises
  • Exercise personalization preferences

This data is accessible only to you and is not synchronized with any external servers.

5. Integrations with External Services

The application uses the following external services:

  • Google AdMob - displaying ads in the application. Google AdMob may collect data in accordance with Google's privacy policy. More information: https://policies.google.com/privacy

The application does not use Firebase, Google Analytics, RevenueCat, or other analytical services. Most app features work offline and do not require an internet connection, except for displaying ads.

6. Data Sharing

Local data: Data stored locally on your device (discomfort level, exercise history, favorite exercises) is not shared with third parties and remains exclusively on your device.

Data from external services: Data collected by Google AdMob is processed in accordance with Google's privacy policy. Google AdMob may share data with its partners for ad display purposes. Details regarding data sharing can be found in Google's privacy policy.

We do not sell or share your personal data with other entities for marketing purposes beyond those necessary for the application's operation (displaying ads).

7. Data Deletion

You can delete all data stored locally on your device at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the application - this will delete all local data
  • Clearing app data in device settings

After deleting the application, all data is permanently deleted from your device and cannot be recovered.

8. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, data security depends on your device's security. We recommend:

  • Using a screen lock on your device
  • Regularly updating the operating system
  • Not sharing the device with unauthorized persons

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The application does not directly use cookies. However, external services (Google AdMob) may use tracking technologies to display personalized ads. Google AdMob may use advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android) and other technologies in accordance with Google's privacy policy.

You can control personalized ads in your device settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy → Ads, Android: Settings → Google → Ads).

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to verify this Privacy Policy. We will inform users about any significant changes by updating the application. Continuing to use the application after changes are introduced means accepting the new policy.

11. Wellbeing Data

All wellbeing data entered by you in the application (discomfort/tension level, exercise history) is stored exclusively locally on your device. It is not transmitted or stored on any external servers. You have full control over this data and can delete it at any time.

12. Compliance with Apple App Store and Google Play Requirements

The application complies with Apple App Store and Google Play guidelines regarding user privacy. We inform users about data collection by external service (Google AdMob) in accordance with the requirements of both stores. The privacy policy is available in the application and online on this page.

In App Store Connect and Google Play Console, we declare the collection of the following data categories by external service:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA/AAID) - by Google AdMob
  • Device technical data - by Google AdMob

13. User Rights (GDPR)

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access - you can check what data is stored locally on your device (available in app settings)
  • Right to erasure - you can delete all local data by uninstalling the application or clearing app data in device settings
  • Right to object - you can disable personalized ads in device settings
  • Right to data portability - local data is stored on your device and can be exported via device backup

In the case of data collected by Google AdMob, you can contact Google directly in accordance with Google's privacy policy.

14. Contact

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact us:

  • Email: contact.fizi.app@gmail.com
  • Via the application
